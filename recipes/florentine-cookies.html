Florentine Cookies

Description

These Florentine lace cookies are simple to make and gorgeous to look at.

This cookie technique is versatile and adaptable to any kinds of nuts or chocolate. They also keep pretty well so they're perfect if you're looking for an idea for an edible holiday gift for any cookie lover on your list--just stack them up in a box and tie them with a bow!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole raw almonds
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • half a cup of honey
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Place almonds into the food processor.
  3. Combine ingredients.
  4. Add vanilla extract.
  5. Place in the center of the oven.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes.
