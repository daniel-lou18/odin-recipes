Meatloaf

Description

This is a very easy and no fail recipe for meatloaf. It won't take long to make at all, and it's quite good! Just follow the steps described below.

Have fun!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Pour sauce over meatloaf.
  4. Bake for 1 hour in preheated oven.
